title: "Individual Identification Numbers by Country"
description: "List of accepted identification number types for individuals by country and region."

The following lists identification numbers that will be accepted for individuals by region.

<Note>
  In addition to the items listed in the table below, `passport` and `national_id` are valid options for all countries **except the USA**.
</Note>
