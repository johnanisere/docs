We support a wide range of fiat and digital currencies. Some of the major currencies include:

### Fiat Currencies

- **USD** (United States Dollar)
- **EUR** (Euro)
- **BRL** (Brazilian Real)
- **NGN** (Nigerian Naira)
- **GHS** (Ghana Cedis)
- **ZMW** (Zambian Kwachas)

### Stablecoins

- **USDC** (USD Coin)
- **USDT** (Tether)

### Cryptocurrencies

- **BTC** (Bitcoin)
