Bullring Finance is a global payments platform that empowers businesses to send and receive payments in multiple currencies, breaking down the barriers of international trade. Whether you're paying suppliers in another country, receiving payments from international customers, or managing a global payroll, our API provides a secure and efficient solution.

This documentation will guide you through our API, helping you to create accounts, manage multi-currency balances, and handle cross-border transactions.
